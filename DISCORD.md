Hey all 👋 I built a little tool that ranks Warframe missions by how much **platinum** they're actually worth to farm. It pulls the official drop table and combines it with live warframe.market sell prices, so you get a ranked leaderboard of which missions pay best — broken down by mission type (Spy, Survival, Defense, Capture, bounties, etc.) with a per-rotation drop breakdown.
